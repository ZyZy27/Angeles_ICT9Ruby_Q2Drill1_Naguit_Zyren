Emergency Go bag Essentials
Emergency Food and Water
Keep atleast one liter of clean water for hydration during emergenices. Pack non-perishable food items such as energy bars and canned goods.
First Aid Kit
Include basic medical supplies like bandages, antiseptic, and pain relievers.
Flashlight and Whistle
Use a reliable flashlight to see clearly during power outages or at night. Use it to signal for help and make rescuers awares of your location.
Extra Clothes
Bring a change of clothes to stay warm and dry during an evacuation.
